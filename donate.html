Make a Difference

Help PAWS Care for Rescued Animals

PAWS depends on public support to provide food, veterinary care, shelter maintenance and daily assistance for animals waiting for adoption.

🍲

Food and Nutrition

Donations help provide daily meals for cats, dogs, puppies and kittens.

💉

Medical Treatment

Financial support helps cover vaccinations, medicines and veterinary treatment.

🏠

Shelter Care

Contributions support cleaning, repairs, equipment and general shelter operations.

Shelter Wishlist

Items You Can Donate

Besides cash contributions, PAWS also welcomes useful items that support everyday shelter care.

🥫

Pet Food

Canned or dry food for puppies, kittens, adult dogs and adult cats.

🧼

Cleaning Supplies

Laundry detergent, bleach, liquid hand soap, paper towels and large garbage bags.

🐾

Animal Care Items

Cat litter, milk replacer, pest preventives, surgical gloves and stainless-steel bowls.

📰

Reusable Materials

Old newspapers and other useful materials for animal cages and transport.

🖨️

Office Supplies

Printer ink and other supplies used for shelter administration.

🪑

Appliances and Furniture

Pre-loved fans, refrigerators, washing machines and suitable shelter furniture.

Item requirements may change. Please contact PAWS before delivering large items or equipment.

Important Safety Notice

Beware of Donation Scams

Verify donation information before transferring money. Be cautious of messages on social media that claim to represent PAWS or promise financial returns.

For verification, contact PAWS using its official phone number or email address.
