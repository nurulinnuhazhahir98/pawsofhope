Give Your Time, Share Your Love

Become a PAWS Volunteer

Volunteering gives you the opportunity to support rescued animals, assist with shelter activities and help create a caring environment for cats and dogs waiting for homes.

View Volunteer Process

Make a Difference

How Volunteers Help PAWS

Volunteers may spend time socialising with animals or assist with suitable enrichment activities at the shelter. Volunteers are expected to work responsibly and independently because supervision during activities may be limited.

🐱

Socialise Cats

Spend time gently interacting with cats and helping them become comfortable around people.

🐕

Walk Dogs

Help selected dogs exercise and enjoy safe outdoor activity according to shelter instructions.

🧹

Support Shelter Care

Assist with appropriate shelter activities that help create a cleaner and more comfortable environment.

Plan Your Visit

Volunteer Times

Volunteers may choose to help for approximately one or two hours. Advance booking is required before visiting.

Thursday – Tuesday

10:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.

Volunteer sessions are available during this period, subject to advance booking and available slots.

Wednesday & Public Holidays

Closed

The shelter is closed for maintenance and volunteer activities are not available.

Before You Visit

Volunteer Process

1

Make an Advance Booking

Call PAWS during shelter office hours before attending. Volunteer slots are limited and offered on a first-come, first-served basis.

2

Complete the Volunteer Form

All new volunteers must complete the appropriate official volunteer registration form before their first session.

3

Receive Confirmation

Attend only after confirming your volunteer slot with PAWS because the shelter can accommodate a limited number of volunteers.

4

Attend Prepared

Wear suitable clothing, follow the shelter guidelines and bring any required personal items.

Information for Students

Student Volunteers

Special conditions apply to student volunteers to ensure safety and prevent overcrowding at the shelter.

01

Weekday Volunteering

Student volunteers may volunteer on weekdays only, except Wednesdays when the shelter is closed.

02

Independent Volunteers

Volunteers must be at least 16 years old to volunteer independently.

03

Junior Volunteers

Volunteers aged 12 to 15 must be supervised by an adult companion for the entire session.

Safety and Preparation

Volunteer Guidelines

👕

Dress Appropriately

Wear a comfortable T-shirt, long pants and closed-toe footwear suitable for shelter activities.

💧

Bring Drinking Water

Bring enough water for yourself and consider carrying a change of clothes in case activities become messy.

🐕

Dog Walking Preparation

Volunteers who walk dogs must bring suitable waste bags and clean up responsibly during the walk.
