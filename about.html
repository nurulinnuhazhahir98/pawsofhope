About PAWS Malaysia

Learn more about our mission, our work, and how we strive to create a better future for abandoned animals.

What is PAWS?

PAWS (PAWS Animal Welfare Society) is a non-profit animal welfare organisation based in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia. Since 1987, PAWS has been dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned, neglected, and unwanted cats and dogs. The organisation provides shelter, vaccinations, medical treatment, neutering, and adoption services to help every rescued animal find a safe and loving home.

PAWS is supported through generous public donations, fundraising events, volunteers, veterinarians, and dedicated staff members. As a registered society in Malaysia, PAWS also promotes responsible pet ownership through educational programmes, community outreach, and animal welfare campaigns.

🎯 Our Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned animals while promoting responsible pet ownership through education, advocacy, and community engagement.

👁 Our Vision

To create a compassionate society where every animal is treated with respect, protected from cruelty, and given the opportunity to live a healthy and happy life.

What We Do

PAWS provides a wide range of services to improve the lives of abandoned animals.

🐱

Animal Rescue

Rescue abandoned, injured, and neglected cats and dogs from unsafe environments.

🏡

Pet Adoption

Match rescued animals with responsible families who can provide lifelong care.

💉

Medical Care

Provide vaccinations, neutering, treatments, and rehabilitation for rescued animals.

📚

Education

Organise awareness programmes to educate the public about responsible pet ownership.

Why PAWS Matters

Every rescued animal deserves a second chance. Through rescue operations, adoption programmes, community education, and volunteer support, PAWS continues to make a meaningful difference in improving animal welfare across Malaysia.
